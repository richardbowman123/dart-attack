PRIVACY POLICY

THE BORING BIT

Dart Attack is a small, independent game. We're not a big company. There's no marketing team. Nobody's selling your data to anyone. Here's what actually happens.

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you sign in, we store:

  • Your email address — so we know who you are and can save your progress
  • Basic gameplay milestones — things like which level you've reached, whether you won or lost, and how your career ended (drink, darts, or dodgy dealings)
  • Survey answers — if we ask you a question in the game and you choose to answer it

That's it. No location tracking, no browsing history, no weird stuff.

WHY WE COLLECT IT

To make the game better. We look at where players get stuck, what they enjoy, and what needs fixing. It helps us prioritise what to build next.

WHO SEES IT

Just us — the people who make the game. We don't share your data with anyone else. No advertisers, no third parties, no bloke down the pub.

HOW LONG WE KEEP IT

As long as the game exists and your account is active. If you stop playing, your data just sits there quietly. It doesn't go anywhere.

WANT IT DELETED?

No hard feelings. Email dartattackgame@gmail.com and ask us to delete your data. We'll wipe everything — email, gameplay history, survey answers — within 30 days.

COOKIES

We don't use tracking cookies. The game uses your browser's local storage to keep you logged in. That's all.

CHANGES

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. We won't suddenly start doing anything dodgy without telling you.

CONTACT

Got a question? dartattackgame@gmail.com
